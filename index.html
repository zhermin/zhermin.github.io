So, let's keep this simple, stupid. Yes, I coded everything you see here.

But.. Why?


Just before your fizzling attention span begs for you to click away — why all the trouble?

Simple, I like coding.


Well, maybe less so when I'm sitting in front of my screen for hours staring at the same error..

Though, I guess to most, this might seem insignificant. Just typing, right? No usage of algorithms, hash tables or hashbrowns.
